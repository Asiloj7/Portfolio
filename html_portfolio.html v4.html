About Me

Jolisa is a proud Trinbagonian transplant currently based in Auburn, Alabama. She brings a vibrant blend of Caribbean warmth and global perspective to everything she does. With an Associate of Arts in Media and a Bachelor of Arts in Mass Communication from the University of Sunderland, she has built a strong foundation in media, storytelling, and communication strategy. She is currently furthering her education by pursuing a Master of Arts in Technical Communication at Auburn University.

A passionate writer at heart, Jolisa is also an avid reader and a naturally effusive communicator. Her enthusiasm for language and storytelling is matched by her analytical mindset and keen eye for detail, which make her a natural problem solver. Over the years, she has developed a unique skill set that blends project management with both technical and creative writing. This combination allows her to craft content that is not only engaging and user-focused but also aligned with organisational goals and deadlines.

Whether she’s managing a project timeline, refining technical documentation, or brainstorming content strategies, Jolisa brings energy, insight, and a collaborative spirit to every endeavor. She’s excited to continue growing as a communicator and to contribute meaningfully to every project she joins.You can find her exploring local eateries, reading a good book or day dreaming about the beach!
