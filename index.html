About Me

Jolisa Brewster is a Trinbagonian professional based in Auburn, Alabama, with a background in media, storytelling, and communication strategy.

She holds a Bachelor of Arts in Mass Communication from the University of Sunderland and is currently pursuing a Master of Arts in Technical Communication at Auburn University.

Her work combines technical writing, creative storytelling, and project management to deliver clear, user-focused content aligned with organizational goals.
