About Me

Hello and welcome! I'm Jolisa, a Trinbagonian professional based in Auburn, Alabama, with a background in media, storytelling, and communication strategy.

I'm currently pursuing a Master in Technical Communication at Auburn University and hold a Bachelor of Arts in Mass Communication from the University of Sunderland.

My work combines technical writing, creative storytelling, and project management to deliver clear, user-focused content aligned with organisational goals.

My mission is to lead a life grounded in integrity and honesty. Through mentorship and leadership, I aim to inspire and empower future generations, guided by a commitment to communication, social advocacy, equity, accountability, and lasting impact.
