About Me

Jolisa is a Trinbagonian creative based in Auburn, Alabama, with a background in meadi, storytelling and communication strategy.

She holds a Bachelor of Arts in Mass Communication from the University of Sunderland and is currently pursuing her Master's in Technical Communication at Auburn University.

Her work blends technical and creative writing, producing content that is enaging, user-focused, and strategically aligned with oraganizations.

Outside of work, she enjoys reading and exploring local spots, and spending time imaginging her next creative project.
