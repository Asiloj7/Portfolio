About Me

Jolisa is a Trinbagonian creative based in Auburn, Alabama, with a background in media, storytelling, and communication strategy.

She holds a Bachelor of Arts in Mass Communication from the University of Sunderland and is currently pursuing a Master’s in Technical Communication at Auburn University.

Her work blends technical and creative writing, producing content that is engaging, user-focused, and strategically aligned.

Outside of work, she enjoys reading, exploring local spaces, and spending time imagining her next creative project.
